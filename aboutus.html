About us

Our mission

At Starlight we know that happiness matters to sick kids facing the pain, fear and stress of serious illness.

That's why for over 30 years we've worked in partnership with health professionals to bring the fun, joy and laughter that helps sick kids be kids.

We help kids of all ages regardless of disability, injury or illness because what happens in childhood lasts a lifetime.

We believe in happiness because it matters to sick kids' health.

Our impact

We continually evaluate and independantly measure the impact of all our national programs.

A study conducted by PWC in 2012 shows that for every $1 invested into our Starlight express Room program, there's a return over $4 to the community. Updated figures in 2018 reflect our extended programs reach and an increased return of $5.70 to the community.

Additionally, Social Ventures reported that for every $1 invested in our Livewire online community for sick teens, %5.30 was returned to the community in value.
